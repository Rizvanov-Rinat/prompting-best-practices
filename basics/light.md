Lighting is the most critical variable for setting the "mood" of your generation. In AI video prompting (Sora, Runway, Pika, Deforum), specifying the **source**, **quality**, and **direction** of light prevents flat, boring results.

## 1. Natural & Time-Based Lighting

### Golden Hour / Magic Hour
The short window right after sunrise or before sunset. The light is warm, soft, golden, and directional.
* **Effect:** Universally flattering, nostalgic, romantic.
* **Best for:** Travel vlogs, nature, beauty shots.

> **Prompts:** `golden hour`, `warm sunlight`, `sunset glow`, `magic hour`, `soft morning light`, `sun flare`, `warm atmosphere`

### Blue Hour
The period just before sunrise or after sunset (twilight). The world looks blue, cold, and tranquil.
* **Effect:** Melancholic, calm, or mysterious.
* **Best for:** Cityscapes, establishing shots, sad scenes.

> **Prompts:** `blue hour`, `cold lighting`, `twilight`, `dusk`, `deep blue tones`, `fading light`, `cool atmosphere`

### Overcast / Flat Light
Cloudy sky acting as a giant diffuser. No hard shadows.
* **Effect:** Soft, even, moody, sometimes depressing.
* **Best for:** Dramas, realistic documentary style.

> **Prompts:** `overcast sky`, `diffused light`, `flat lighting`, `cloudy day`, `soft shadows`

## 2. Cinematic Techniques

### Volumetric Lighting / God Rays
Light beams visible in the air due to dust, fog, or smoke.
* **Effect:** Epic, holy, dusty, adds depth to 3D space.
* **Best for:** Forests, cathedrals, abandoned buildings, cinematic reveals.

> **Prompts:** `volumetric lighting`, `god rays`, `light shafts`, `dusty atmosphere`, `foggy light`, `tyndall effect`, `cinematic haze`

### Rim Light / Backlighting
The light source is placed *behind* the subject.
* **Effect:** Creates a glowing outline (halo) around the silhouette, separating the subject from the background.
* **Best for:** Epic hero shots, mystery, separating dark hair from a dark background.

> **Prompts:** `rim light`, `backlighting`, `silhouette`, `halo effect`, `strong backlight`, `edge lighting`, `hair light`

### Rembrandt Lighting (Chiaroscuro)
A specific side-lighting technique that creates a triangle of light on the shadowed cheek.
* **Effect:** High contrast, dramatic, serious, artistic.
* **Best for:** Villains, dramatic dialogue, classic portraits.

> **Prompts:** `Rembrandt lighting`, `chiaroscuro`, `side lighting`, `high contrast`, `dramatic shadows`, `half-lit face`

### Cinematic / Studio Lighting
Controlled, deliberate lighting (often 3-point lighting).
* **Effect:** Polished, high-quality, commercial look.
* **Best for:** Interviews, product showcases, fashion.

> **Prompts:** `cinematic lighting`, `studio lighting`, `professional lighting`, `softbox`, `4k quality`

## 3. Stylized & Artificial Lighting

### Neon / Cyberpunk
Artificial, highly saturated colored lights (usually pinks, cyans, and purples).
* **Effect:** Futuristic, high-energy, synthetic.
* **Best for:** Sci-fi, nightlife, music videos.

> **Prompts:** `neon lighting`, `cyberpunk lights`, `cyan and magenta lighting`, `fluorescent light`, `glowwave`, `night city lights`

### Bioluminescence
Natural light emitted by living organisms (glow-in-the-dark).
* **Effect:** Magical, ethereal, alien.
* **Best for:** Fantasy, underwater scenes, Avatar-style worlds.

> **Prompts:** `bioluminescence`, `glowing plants`, `ethereal glow`, `luminescent forest`, `magical atmosphere`, `night glow`

---

## 4. Light Quality (Shadows)

* **Soft Light:** Shadows are blurry or non-existent. Smooths out skin textures.
    * > `soft lighting`, `diffused light`, `gentle glow`
* **Hard Light:** Shadows have sharp, defined edges. Emphasizes texture and grit.
    * > `hard lighting`, `harsh shadows`, `direct sunlight`, `sharp contrast`, `flashlight`
