This section defines the overall "look and feel" of the generated video. Specifying a style ensures the AI doesn't default to a generic digital look.

> **Pro Tip:** You can mix styles (e.g., "Cyberpunk Claymation") to create unique results, but keep the prompt structured to avoid confusion.

## 1. Photorealism & Cinematic
The goal is to make the footage indistinguishable from real life or a high-budget movie.
* **Photorealistic:** Focuses on texture, skin details, and realistic lighting.
* **Cinematic:** Focuses on composition, color grading (Teal & Orange), and dramatic mood.

> **Prompts:** `photorealistic`, `8k resolution`, `raw footage`, `hyperrealistic`, `shot on IMAX`, `film grain`, `unreal engine 5 render`, `cinematic lighting`, `color graded`, `depth of field`

## 2. 3D Animation & CGI
Styles that look like modern video games or animated movies.
* **Pixar/Disney Style:** Cute proportions, bright lighting, "squash and stretch" motion.
* **High-End Render:** Looks like a product commercial or sci-fi game cinematic.

> **Prompts:** `3D render`, `Octane render`, `Unreal Engine 5`, `Pixar style`, `Disney animation`, `CGI`, `ray tracing`, `blender 3d`, `smooth textures`, `volumetric fog`

## 3. Anime & 2D Illustration
Hand-drawn or cel-shaded styles typical of Japanese animation or comic books.
* **Studio Ghibli:** Lush backgrounds, food, nature, peaceful vibes.
* **90s Anime:** Retro aesthetic, lower frame rate feel, grain.

> **Prompts:** `anime style`, `Studio Ghibli`, `Makoto Shinkai`, `cel shaded`, `2D animation`, `hand drawn`, `manga style`, `vibrant colors`, `thick outlines`

## 4. Analog & Retro Video
Imitating old camera technology to create nostalgia or horror ("Found Footage").
* **VHS:** Tape distortion, glitch lines, low resolution.
* **Vintage Film:** 16mm or 35mm film grain, scratches, sepia tones.

> **Prompts:** `VHS glitch`, `1990s footage`, `camcorder style`, `faded colors`, `film grain`, `16mm film`, `vintage texture`, `distorted video`, `CRT monitor effect`

## 5. Claymation & Stop Motion
Physical, tactile textures that look like clay, plastic, or puppets moving frame-by-frame.

> **Prompts:** `claymation`, `stop motion`, `Aardman style`, `plasticine texture`, `Laika style`, `handmade`, `miniature set`, `tactile texture`, `jerky movement`

## 6. Cyberpunk & Sci-Fi
Futuristic, high-tech, dystopic visuals.

> **Prompts:** `cyberpunk`, `vaporwave`, `neon city`, `futuristic`, `holographic`, `chrome textures`, `synthwave`, `retro-futurism`, `glitch art`

## 7. Low Poly & Pixel Art
Digital aesthetics that intentionally use low resolution or simple geometry.

> **Prompts:** `low poly`, `PlayStation 1 graphics`, `pixel art`, `8-bit`, `16-bit`, `blocky textures`, `minecraft style`, `voxel art`

## 8. Traditional Art & Painting
Mimics physical art media using brush strokes and canvas textures.
* **Oil Painting:** Thick, textured strokes (Impasto).
* **Watercolor:** Soft, bleeding edges, pastel colors.
* **Ink/Sketch:** High contrast, black and white lines.

> **Prompts:** `oil painting`, `impasto brush strokes`, `watercolor style`, `wet on wet`, `ink sketch`, `charcoal drawing`, `Van Gogh style`, `canvas texture`, `concept art`

## 9. Papercraft & Origami
The world is made entirely of paper, cardboard, or craft materials. Great for explainer videos or cute aesthetics.

> **Prompts:** `paper cutout`, `origami style`, `layered paper`, `cardboard texture`, `diorama`, `craft materials`, `paper art`, `folded paper`

## 10. Comic Book & Graphic Novel
Distinctive outlines, shading, and halftone patterns.
* **Noir:** High contrast black and white, dramatic shadows.
* **Modern Comic:** Vibrant colors, strong ink lines.

> **Prompts:** `comic book style`, `graphic novel`, `Marvel style`, `halftone pattern`, `thick ink lines`, `speech bubbles`, `Sin City style`, `noir illustration`

## 11. Dark Fantasy & Gothic
Moody, supernatural, and often unsettling visuals. Useful for horror or medieval fantasy.
* **Eldritch:** Lovecraftian, tentacles, fog, insanity.
* **Dark Souls Style:** Ruined castles, grey tones, heavy armor.

> **Prompts:** `dark fantasy`, `gothic style`, `H.R. Giger`, `eldritch horror`, `gloomy atmosphere`, `foggy`, `ruins`, `mystical`, `grimdark`

## 12. Abstract & Surreal
Non-representational visuals, fluid simulations, or dreamlike imagery.
* **Fluid:** Liquid metal, smoke, water simulations.
* **Psychedelic:** Fractals, kaleidoscopes, melting objects.

> **Prompts:** `abstract art`, `fluid simulation`, `liquid metal`, `fractal`, `psychedelic`, `dreamscape`, `melting reality`, `double exposure`, `surrealism`
